Agnes Wants To Die

A digital puzzle box experience

What is it?

Agnes Wants To Die is a narrative puzzle-box experience. But what does that mean? Imagine an escape room in the form of a webapp. That's pretty much it.

How does it work?

There are a few chapters, each with an entirely different puzzle. To understand each puzzle and to see hints and context, you will need to scour the 'emails' from a specific character. Make sure not to move away from the page, progress is only saved as long as you STAY ON THE PUZZLE PAGE. You should record your answers on paper before submitting them, just so you don't lose answers to puzzles you've already solved.
